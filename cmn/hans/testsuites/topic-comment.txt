Comment: This construction is sometimes called "Possessive-Raising" (i.e., Zhangsan's height is tall.) (Ura, 1996).  It may have two different readings.

张三 个子 很 高 。
Zhang1san1 ge4zi hen3 gao1 .
Zhangsan height very tall .
As for Zhangsan, he is very tall. / Zhangsan’s stature is very tall.
